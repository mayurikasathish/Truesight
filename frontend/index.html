Generate Keypair

Generate a new Ed25519 cryptographic keypair. Your private key signs media, while the public key allows anyone to verify authenticity.

Watermark Media

Embed an invisible cryptographic signature into your image or video. The watermark proves authenticity and detects tampering.

💡 Note: The watermark embeds a 512-bit Ed25519 signature using DWT-QIM. Save your keys securely for future verification!
✅

Verify Watermark

Extract and verify the cryptographic watermark from an image or video. Requires the watermarked media, original media, and public key.

⚠️ Important: Both the watermarked AND original media are required because the signature was computed from the original image bytes.
